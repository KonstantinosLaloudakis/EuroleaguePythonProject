# Shot Creation Network: Greek Derby Analysis
## Olympiacos vs Panathinaikos — Euroleague 2025-26

---

## Overview
The **Shot Creation Network** combines spatial expected Field Goal percentage (xFG%) with play-by-play assist data to build a web of who creates the highest quality shots for whom. 

- **xFG%** is calculated based on the shot's zone, distance from the basket, and shot type.
- **Assist Connections** track the volume and quality of looks generated by a specific passer for a specific scorer.

### Elite Connections
- **The Engine:** **Walkup &rarr; Vezenkov** is the highest volume connection (63 Assists) and generates elite shot quality (**57.0% xFG**).
- **The Lob Threat:** **Fournier &rarr; Hall** is a deadly connection, generating **67.1% xFG** across 13 assists. Fournier also connects with Milutinov at an elite **67.3% xFG** (9 assists).

### Key Takeaway for OLY
**Thomas Walkup** is the undisputed floor general in terms of volume, effectively conducting the offense and finding Vezenkov. However, **Evan Fournier** operates as the ultimate gravity well—when he passes, he generates the highest quality looks on the team, especially for the rolling big men (Hall and Milutinov).

### Elite Connections
- **The Pick & Roll Masterclass:** **Sloukas &rarr; Yurtseven** is the most efficient interior connection, creating a staggering **65.3% xFG** across 20 assists.
- **The Highlight Reel:** **Sloukas &rarr; Faried** (14 assists) and **Nunn &rarr; Faried** (15 assists) generate an unbelievable **~80% xFG**, mostly comprised of dunks and alley-oops. 
- **The Spacing Connection:** **Sloukas &rarr; Hernangomez** generates great volume (26 Assists) but slightly lower quality (**53.0% xFG**), reflecting Hernangomez's perimeter-oriented shot diet.

### Key Takeaway for PAN
**Kostas Sloukas** is the heartbeat of the offense, manipulating the pick-and-roll to spoon-feed Yurtseven and Faried for ultra-high percentage looks. Similar to Fournier for OLY, **Kendrick Nunn** creates the highest quality looks when he decides to pass (**60.7% xFG**), punishing defenses that over-commit to his scoring threat.

---

## The Verdict

Both teams feature a fascinating dichotomy between their pure point guards (Walkup, Sloukas) driving the sheer volume of the offense and their elite scoring wings (Fournier, Nunn) generating the highest quality looks when creating for others. 

Panathinaikos's network features higher spikes in shot quality on interior connections (Faried, Yurtseven), whereas Olympiacos relies on the relentless machinery of the Walkup-Vezenkov connection to wear down opponents.

### Elite Connections
- **The Core Engine:** **Montero &rarr; Reuvers** is the highest volume and most efficient primary connection (30 Assists, **64.3% xFG**). Thompson also heavily mirrors this, feeding Reuvers for 22 assists.
- **The Secondary Hub:** **Moore &rarr; Costello** generates solid looks (17 Assists, **53.5% xFG**) entirely from the perimeter (8 three-pointers vs 6 two-pointers).
- **The Pure Finisher:** **Thompson &rarr; Sako** is low volume (10 assists) but generates incredibly high-quality interior looks (**78.7% xFG**).

### Key Takeaway for PAM
Valencia features a distinct "**Two-Headed Monster**" in Montero and Thompson. They both create identically efficient overall shot quality (54.6% xFG) and heavily target the same primary big man (Nathan Reuvers). The offense flows heavily through guard penetration resulting in kick-outs or dump-offs to high-efficiency finishers.
